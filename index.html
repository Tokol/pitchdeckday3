Opportunity

Untapped younger consumer segment.

The opportunity is not only selling local food to existing buyers. It is expanding the category through younger appeal, ecosystem creation, and regional resilience.

Younger consumer appeal Growing awareness around food origin, sustainability, freshness, and local impact.
New local ecosystem Connect producers, consumers, pickup points, communities, and local delivery partners.
Food security & resilience Global instability, war, climate disruption, and supply-chain shocks make local systems strategic.
